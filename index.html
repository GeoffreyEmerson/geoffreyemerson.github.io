About

Geoffrey Emerson is a full stack web developer currently residing in Portland, Oregon. He started programming for fun at a very young age, and in 2015 decided to make the switch from hobbyist to professional. Geoff is currently focusing on Node.js backend services, but he also dabbles in React frontends.

Over the years he has also studied Basic, Pascal, C, C++, C#, Objective-C, PHP, and Ruby on Rails. While he took many programming classes over the years, his Bachelor's Degree is in Theatre Arts with an emphasis on Acting and Musical Theatre.
